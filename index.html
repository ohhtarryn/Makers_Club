Makers Club meets the first Tuesday of every month at Art Marble 21 at 8:00pm.

Makers Club is for people who enjoy making things. Artists, designers, architects, illustrators, typographers, filmmakers, photographers, programmers, everyone is welcome. There is no agenda, it's not for recruiting, the point is just to meet, talk, and drink with other people.
